Essa's Rambling club

The Ramblers Association has been active in promoting better access to and protection of the country since the 1930s. Today, walking has become a very popular pastime with young and old alike. In order to cater for the vast numbers of walkers who wish to enjoy the countryside for longer periods at a time there is now a myriad of accommodation for the discerning rambler to choose from.
